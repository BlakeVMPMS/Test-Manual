Make a Reservation

Make a reservation from scratch. Start by clicking Front Office.

  • The first tab in Front Office is Reservations. When you click through Reservations, you are on a blank reservation screen.
  • If you search for a reservation, click the Add Res function icon (on the left side of the screen).

Systematically enter information into each field to make a reservation.

  • Not all fields are required, but you need to know even the least-used fields.
  • Refer back to this section should you need a refresher on these important front-desk fields. Each field corresponds to another section in the Visual Matrix application and bears weight.

Complete the following steps to make a reservation.

  1. Enter the guestâ€™s Last and First Name. Skip the Guest ID No. for now -- you can use the number to easily look up guests who have stayed at the hotel.
  2. Enter the Arrival date, or select the date from the calendar icon. This is the date that the guest plans to check in to the hotel. Enter the Departure date, the check out date; or, enter the number of Nights the guest will stay.
  3. For example: Arrival: 1/1 + Nights: 2 = Check out date: 1/3
  4. Enter the # of Rooms. Guests can book more than one room. Using folios, you can enter different guest names for each room, if necessary.
